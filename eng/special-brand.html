  • INFO of ALISTAR COFFEE

Withus Healthcare Co., Ltd. joins
the beautiful journey of ALISTAR Coffee
for a better tomorrow in Baringo, Kenya.

In the Baringo region of Kenya, Africa
"85% of residents live in absolute poverty."

CEO Kim Jung-hwa and Director Yoo Eun-seong, who currently lead ALISTAR Coffee Co., Ltd., began planting coffee trees for the farmers of Baringo 12 years ago.

With the goal of improving the quality of life for Baringo farmers, they have supplied approximately 6,500 computers as of 2020 and continue to purchase coffee at fair prices through international fair trade.

By sourcing fresh, high-quality coffee you can trust, ALISTAR Coffee provide it to our customers at reasonable prices.

Farmers in Baringo, Kenya…
"Cultivate coffee in small family gardens."

Coffee enthusiasts believe that coffee grown in high-altitude regions—above 1,200 meters—offers the most exceptional flavor. The Baringo region of Kenya sits at an average altitude of 2,250 meters. As a high-altitude mountainous area, it boasts a breathtaking natural environment, Lake Baringo and beautiful national parks.

Kenya AA coffee beans ripen to a rich red under Baringo's abundant sunlight. Because it is a pristine, pollution-free region, its coffee possesses a distinct flavor and aroma that truly sets it apart from other regions.

Farmers in Baringo do not use chemical fertilizers or pesticides. Even on steep slopes exceeding 50 degrees, entire families come together to manage these small-scale garden farms. By tending to each tree with care and devotion, they harvest only the most premium coffee cherries.

In partnership with the NGO World Best Friend

World Best Friend, the NGO currently partnering with ALISTAR Coffee Co., Ltd., has visited local farms in Baringo, Kenya, to study coffee production methods and distribution structures to eradicate poverty. They have launched a direct-trade fair trade system using ICT to ensure fair profits are returned to Baringo farmers.

The Benefits of Direct-Trade Fair Trade

By participating in direct-trade fair trade, Kenyan farmers have seen their incomes increase by 6 to 20 times. A portion of the profits generated through this system is donated to support the sustainable development of Baringo and the education of local children.

Value Beyond Coffee

ALISTAR Coffee is not just a simple commodity. It is a masterpiece created by the harmony of the people and the nature of Baringo, Kenya.

Each cup transforms the lives of the local community and cultivates sustainable value.

ALISTAR, 'The Noble Star'

The name ALISTAR is a blend of the Ancient Greek word 'ALINA' (meaning noble) and the English word 'STAR', signifying a 'Noble Star'. We are a brand dedicated to connecting people, communities, and values through the world's finest coffee.

Delivered with Expertise
and Sincerity

ALISTAR selects coffee based on professional standards, backed by the Barista Skills Professional and Brewing Professional certifications from the SCA (Specialty Coffee Association), the world's largest coffee organization.

We maintain the highest quality by directly sourcing green beans from Baringo, Kenya, and roasting them in-house.

Connecting the World Through Coffee

ALISTAR goes beyond being just a coffee brand, pursuing the value of growing together with local communities.

We support the economic and educational development of the Baringo region in Kenya, starting with the distribution of coffee trees.
